A good story encourages us to turn the next page and read more. We want to find out what happens next and 
what the main characters do and what they say to each other. We may feel excited, sad, afraid, angry or really happy. 
This is because the experience of reading or listening to a story is much more likely to make us 'feel' that we are part
 of the story, too. Just like in our 'real' lives, we might love or hate different characters
